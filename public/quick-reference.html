The Formal Duel
1
The Challenge
Announce lineage & grievance. Taunt roll — winner gains +1 to first attack (or −1 on opponent). On loss for refusal without cause.
2
The Compact
Agree terms: first blood / submission / death. Confirm weapons and witnesses. Violation costs 3 On immediately.
3
The Combat
Standard SWADE. No Gang Up. No interference (costs 3 On). Iai-jutsu in sheathed-start duels acts before all Action Cards in Round 1.
Spare loser: winner +1 On; loser +1 On. Kill when terms = first blood: −1 On.

Tier 3 — Nippon Detailed System
Step 1:Social Status = On mod + Group Rank mod. Higher starts with 1 free Momentum.
Step 2:Disposition: Hostile (target +2) · Uncooperative (+1) · Neutral · Friendly (petitioner +1) · Helpful (+2).
Step 3:Preparation: gift (+1 step) · go-between (+1) · prior favor (+1–2 steps).
Rounds:Both choose approach, roll opposed, winner gains 1 Mom (raise = 2).
Threshold:3 (moderate) · 5 (significant) · 7 (landmark).
