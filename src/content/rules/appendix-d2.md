These beings originate in legend, in the spirit world, or at the intersection of the mortal and divine. Fighting them is often not the correct solution. Understanding what they want and why they are present usually is.

A note on permanence: most supernatural beings in this appendix cannot be permanently destroyed through combat alone. Exorcism drives Yurei away temporarily. Oni slain in the mortal world may return. Kami banished from their home will eventually reconstitute. The adventure is rarely about defeating these beings — it is about understanding them well enough to resolve the underlying situation.

---

## Legendary Beings

These are not mere humans or ordinary animals. Legendary beings have achieved a status beyond the normal — masters of their craft to the point of legend, supernatural champions, or beings standing at the intersection of the mortal and divine.

---

### [WC] Master Swordsman (Kensei)
*Wild Card — Legendary Bushi*

**Attributes:** Agility d10, Smarts d8, Spirit d10, Strength d10, Vigor d10
**Skills:** Athletics d8, Fighting d12+2, Intimidation d10, Notice d10, Riding d10, Shooting d10, Socialize d8, Warfare d10
**Pace** 6; **Parry** 11; **Toughness** 12 (2); **On** 16
**Hindrances:** —
**Edges:** Edged Weapons (Omote), Kenjutsu, Iai-jutsu, Nito-kenjutsu, Shoshin, Combat Reflexes, Block (Imp), Counterattack (Imp), First Strike (Imp), Kensei
**Gear:** Master Katana (Str+d6+3, AP 2, Fighting +1, Parry +2 — a named blade); Wakizashi (Str+d6); Good Light Samurai armor.
**Special Abilities:**
- **Ki Adept:** 15 Ki PP; all Ki powers.
- **Shoshin:** Three actions at −1 each; a free counter per round when missed.
- **Iai-jutsu:** Draw and full attack as single action; +2 damage vs. unaware targets.
- **Nito-kenjutsu:** Dual Katana and Wakizashi; off-hand Parry +1; second attack at −2.
- **Kensei:** Parry +1; a raise on melee upgrades the damage die.
- **Legendary Presence:** Raw soldiers may make a Fear check at −2 on first confrontation.

*Meeting them socially is an honor. Fighting them is a choice made only by the desperate, the foolish, or the supremely confident. They are likely a potential teacher of Shoshin-level Zanshin — finding them is itself an adventure.*

*Wild Card — Legendary supernatural being*

**Skills:** Athletics d10, Intimidation d8, Notice d12, Perform d12, Socialize d12, Stealth d12, Shinten d8
**Pace** 8; **Parry** 5; **Toughness** 7
**Hindrances:** —
**Edges:** Attractive (Imp), Charismatic, Connections
**Gear:** —
**Special Abilities:**
- **Powers:** *illusion* (at will, free), *puppet* (charm variant), *bolt* (fox fire, free action). **Power Points:** 15
- **Charm:** Opposed Shinten vs. Spirit; success makes the target believe the Kitsune is a trusted old friend.

*Not evil but never straightforwardly helpful. A Kitsune has taken an interest in the party for reasons that seem simple and become complicated. They regard deception as an art form and have genuinely warm feelings toward humans — expressed through elaborate manipulation.*

## Yurei — The Unquiet Dead

A Yurei is a person who died with strong unresolved emotion — grief, rage, unrequited love, betrayal — and returned as a spirit unable to rest. They are tied to a specific place, person, or unresolved situation. Permanently resolving a Yurei requires addressing the underlying cause, not destroying the ghost.

---

### Yurei — Common Ghost
*Extra — Supernatural (undead spirit)*

**Attributes:** Agility d6, Smarts d6, Spirit d10, Strength d6, Vigor d6
**Skills:** Intimidation d10, Notice d12, Stealth d12
**Pace** 6; **Parry** 5; **Toughness** 5
**Special Abilities:**
- **Chill Touch:** If the Yurei manifests, touch deals 2d4 cold damage; target makes Spirit roll or gains 1 Fatigue.
- **Ethereal:** Invisible and immaterial at will. Only affected by magical attacks, blessed weapons, or *exorcism*.
- **Fear (−2):** Appearing visibly causes Fear checks at −2.
- **Kegare Aura:** +1 Kegare per hour of exposure to nearby Shinto Gakusho.
- **Unfinished Business:** Cannot be permanently destroyed until its unresolved situation is addressed. *Exorcism* drives it away for 24 hours only.
- **Wailing:** Once per scene, all within 10" make Fear checks at −2.

*The best outcome of a Yurei encounter is discovering what holds the spirit to the mortal world and resolving it. The worst outcome is a party reduced to Exhausted and Shaken in a haunted building while the Yurei reforms at midnight.*

## Oni — The Demons of Yomi

Oni are supernatural beings from Yomi who cross into the mortal world to spread suffering, disease, and disaster. Buddhist miracles of compassion — *sanctuary*, *relief*, *healing* — do not affect Oni. *Exorcism*, *smite* (holy), and Holy Aura work normally.

---

### Oni — Minor (Kooni)
*Extra — Supernatural (demon)*

**Attributes:** Agility d6, Smarts d6, Spirit d8, Strength d10, Vigor d10
**Skills:** Athletics d8, Fighting d8, Intimidation d8, Notice d6
**Pace** 6; **Parry** 6; **Toughness** 10 (2)
**Edges:** Frenzy
**Gear:** Iron club — Kanabo (Str+d8, AP 2).
**Special Abilities:**
- **Armor +2:** Demonic hide.
- **Claws/Horns:** Str+d6.
- **Demon Nature:** Immune to *sanctuary*, *relief*, and *healing* powers. *Exorcism*, *smite* (holy trapping), Holy Aura, and Spear Against Spirits work normally.
- **Fear (−1):** Oni presence causes Fear checks at −1.
- **Hardy:** A second Shaken result doesn't cause a Wound.
- **Kegare Aura:** +1 Kegare per round of exposure to nearby Shinto Gakusho.
- **Pollution Aura:** +1 Pollution per round of exposure to nearby Buddhist Gakusho.

*Minor Oni bring disease to livestock, poison wells, and brutalize travelers. In organized groups they form effective warbands.*

## Kami — Spirits of the Natural World

Kami cannot be treated as generic monsters. Each Kami is the spirit of a specific thing — a specific river, a specific mountain, a specific ancient tree. A Kami encountered in conflict is almost always a symptom of human wrongdoing. *Exorcism* works but should be a last resort — it is a violation of the Kami, not a resolution of the problem.

---

### Minor Kami (Hearth, Tree, Stream)
*Extra — Supernatural (spirit, typically non-hostile)*

**Attributes:** Agility d6, Smarts d8, Spirit d8, Strength d6, Vigor d6
**Skills:** Notice d10, Shinten d6
**Pace** 6; **Parry** 3; **Toughness** 5
**Special Abilities:**
- **Domain:** Bound to a specific feature within 50". Cannot leave this area.
- **Elemental Influence:** Within its domain, creates minor environmental effects as a free action.
- **Ethereal (usually):** Invisible and intangible; manifests briefly as rustling leaves, rippling water, or warmth near a cold hearth.
- **No Combat:** Does not fight. An offended minor Kami causes misfortune — crop failure, illness, tools breaking — rather than direct harm.
- **Powers:** *elemental manipulation* (domain element only, no PP cost). **Power Points:** 5
- **Propitiation:** Soothed with correct ritual. An appeased Kami becomes actively beneficial.

*The neighbors of everyday Nipponese life. Most people never see them directly, only feel their presence. A Shinto Gakusho can speak with them directly and often resolve problems that would otherwise escalate.*

**Skills:** Athletics d10, Fighting d10, Intimidation d12, Notice d12, Shinten d8
**Pace** 8; **Parry** 7; **Toughness** 10
**Hindrances:** —
**Edges:** Combat Reflexes, Frenzy
**Gear:** —

## Supernatural Beasts

These creatures are more than natural animals — they are beings touched by elemental or spiritual force, capable of effects beyond ordinary wildlife.

---

### Raiju — Thunder Beast
*Extra — Supernatural (elemental creature)*

**Attributes:** Agility d12, Smarts d6 (A), Spirit d8, Strength d8, Vigor d8
**Skills:** Athletics d12, Fighting d10, Notice d10, Stealth d6
**Pace** 12; **Parry** 7; **Toughness** 8
**Edges:** Fleet-Footed, Frenzy
**Special Abilities:**
- **Armor Bypass:** Lightning attacks ignore 4 points of Armor.
- **Lightning Form:** Appears as a creature of lightning — wolf, weasel, or cat.
- **Lightning Leap:** Free action; teleport to any visible point within 20", leaving a lightning streak.
- **Lightning Strike:** Str+d8 electrical; on a raise, target makes Vigor roll or is Stunned.
- **Storm Speed:** In thunderstorms, Pace becomes 24.
- **Weakness:** In clear skies, Pace drops to 6 and all rolls suffer −2.

*Not malicious — simply elementally chaotic. During thunderstorms, becomes erratic and may attack anything in its path. Dealing with one usually means waiting for the storm to pass or finding a way to calm it.*

**Pace** 8; **Parry** 8; **Toughness** 20 (4)

## Using These Stat Blocks

**These are baselines, not limits.** A veteran Ashigaru with Zanshin is dramatically more dangerous than the basic entry. A Kitsune who has guarded a family for three generations may be Friendly rather than unpredictable. Always consider who these beings are in context.

**For human NPCs,** add Profession Edges, Bugei Okuden, and On scores appropriate to their background. A Samurai retainer guarding a Daimyo's person should have significantly higher stats than the baseline here.

**For supernatural beings,** remember that combat is rarely the only option and often not the best one. Nippon rewards cleverness, spiritual knowledge, and understanding the world's deeper nature. A party that fights every supernatural encounter will exhaust their resources quickly; one that engages with the spirit world thoughtfully will find allies and solutions the sword cannot reach.
