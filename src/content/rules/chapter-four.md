Your Profession is your path through Nippon's world. It determines what you are trained to do, which organizations will accept you, how society regards you, and what techniques become available as you advance. Professions are not jobs — they are identities, with obligations and privileges attached.

Each Profession has a full advancement chain from Novice through Legendary rank. Profession Edges are available only to characters of the appropriate Profession. They represent the specialized mastery that comes from dedicated study within a tradition — techniques that cannot be purchased from outside.

All characters begin at Novice rank. Rank advances with Advances earned through play, per standard SWADE rules.

## The Bushi — Warrior of the Buke

The Bushi is the warrior of the samurai class — trained from childhood in the martial arts, bound by obligation to their lord, and measured by the twin standards of martial excellence and social honor. Bushi are the most common Profession among Buke characters and the backbone of every clan's military force.

**Starting Skills (free):** Fighting d6, Riding d4, Shooting d4, Socialize d4

**Starting Bugei (free):** Edged Weapons (Omote) from Birth, plus Kenjutsu (Specialist) from Profession

**On Modifier:** +0

**Ki Focus:** Bushi begin with a dedicated Ki Power Point pool of 5 PP. This pool is separate from standard Power Points and is used only for Ki powers. Ki powers are activated as a free action once per round.

### Ki Powers

Ki powers represent the focused inner force that martial training develops. They are available to all Bushi and Budoka through the Ki Focus Edge.

| Ki Power | Cost | Effect |
|---|---|---|
| Damage Focus | 1–3 PP | +1/+2/+3 damage on melee attacks this round |
| Defense Focus | 1–3 PP | +1/+2/+3 Parry this round |
| Swift Step | 1–3 PP | +2/+4/+6 Pace this round |

Ki PP recover at 1 PP per hour of rest, or fully after a full night's sleep.

### Bushi Advancement Chain

**Novice**
- **Mushin** — Reduces multi-action penalty from −2/−4 to −1/−2. The beginning of the Zanshin chain. Available to all Professions.
- **Warrior's Heart** — May attempt to recover from Shaken as a free action once per round (Spirit roll at no penalty).
- **Ki Focus** — Gain the 5-point Ki PP pool and access to all three Ki powers.

**Seasoned**
- **Zanshin** — Requires Mushin and a qualified teacher. Multi-action penalty becomes −1/−2 with two/three actions (same as Mushin, but the next step in the chain unlocks). Also grants: once per round when an opponent misses with a melee attack, you may immediately make a free Fighting roll against them.
- **Iron Will** — +2 to Spirit rolls to resist Fear, Intimidation, and supernatural compulsion.
- **Battlefield Awareness** — Never Surprised. If allies would be Surprised, you may make a Notice roll to warn them (they are still Surprised on a failure).

**Veteran**
- **Fudoshin** — Requires Zanshin and a qualified teacher. Multi-action penalty becomes −0/−1 with two/three actions. The immovable mind.
- **Living Weapon** — May use Ki PP to add +1 damage per PP spent (max 3) to unarmed strikes. Counts as a natural weapon — ignores the unarmed combat penalty.
- **Combat Veteran** — Once per combat, when Incapacitated, make a Vigor roll (TN 4). With success, remain at 1 Wound instead of going down.

**Heroic**
- **Shoshin** — Requires Fudoshin and a qualified teacher. Three actions at −0/−1 each. Additionally, once per round when any attacker misses you in melee, make a free Fighting roll against them. The beginner's mind — open to everything, fixed on nothing.
- **Clan Champion** — +2 to all trait rolls when fighting in defense of your lord, clan, or sworn allies. Must be in direct, visible defense — not ambush or assassination.

**Legendary**
- **Zanshin Kanzen** — Requires Shoshin and a campaign-level quest to find a teacher. All multi-action penalties eliminated. May take any number of actions in a round at no penalty (still limited to SWADE action economy rules). The complete mastery of combat flow.
- **Legend of the Sword** — Your reputation precedes you. All enemies who know your name must make a Fear check (−2) before engaging you in single combat for the first time.

## The Budoka — Martial Artist

The Budoka is the unarmed fighter and martial arts specialist — the practitioner of a Ryu who has dedicated their life to mastering the body as a weapon. Budoka range from disciplined temple students to wandering fighters who sell their skills, to the enforcers of criminal organizations.

**Starting Skills (free):** Athletics d6, Fighting d6, Notice d4

**Starting Bugei (free):** Unarmed Combat (Omote) from Birth or Profession, plus Atemi-waza (Specialist) from Profession

**On Modifier:** −1

**Ki Focus:** Budoka share the Bushi's Ki Focus system with the same 5-point pool and identical Ki powers.

### Budoka Advancement Chain

**Novice**
- **Mushin** — As Bushi.
- **Ki Focus** — As Bushi.
- **Iron Body** — Toughness +1 when unarmored. Unarmed strikes deal Str+d6 (already factored through Atemi-waza).

**Seasoned**
- **Zanshin** — As Bushi. Requires teacher.
- **Dim Mak — Novice** — Called Shot to a specific body location at −2. With a raise, the target suffers a Wound-level penalty to actions using that location for 24 hours (arm: −2 Fighting; leg: −2 Pace and Agility; torso: −1 all physical actions).
- **Iron Skin** — Toughness +2 when unarmored (replaces Iron Body's +1). Counts as natural Armor +2 against unarmed attacks.

**Veteran**
- **Fudoshin** — As Bushi. Requires teacher.
- **Dim Mak — Veteran** — Requires Dim Mak (Novice). Called Shot penalty reduced to 0. On a raise, the location penalty is permanent until treated by a healer with Healing d8+ (TN 8 roll).
- **Living Weapon** — As Bushi.

**Heroic**
- **Shoshin** — As Bushi. Requires teacher.
- **Dim Mak — Heroic** — Requires Dim Mak (Veteran). With a raise on the attack, the GM makes a secret Vigor roll for the target. Failure means the target will die within 1d6 days unless treated. Success means the standard Veteran penalty applies instead.

**Legendary**
- **Zanshin Kanzen** — As Bushi. Requires quest.
- **One Strike, One Death** — Once per combat, declare before rolling. If the attack hits with a raise, the target makes a Vigor roll (TN 8). Failure means Incapacitation regardless of Toughness or Wounds.

## The Gakusho (Buddhist) — Buddhist Monk or Priest

The Buddhist Gakusho is a religious practitioner of the Buddhist tradition — monk, priest, or temple scholar. They command divine power through Butsudo, serve their temple hierarchy, and navigate a world where religious authority and political power are deeply intertwined.

**Starting Skills (free):** Butsudo d6, Healing d4, Knowledge (Buddhism) d6, Socialize d4

**Starting Bugei (free):** None from Profession (Gakusho are not martial by default). Birth Bugei applies normally.

**Arcane Background (Miracles: Buddhist):** Butsudo d4 free, 3 starting powers from Buddhist list, 10 PP.

**On Modifier:** +2

### Gakusho Ki

Gakusho of both traditions may take the Gakusho Ki Edge (Seasoned), which grants a 5-point Ki pool separate from their Power Points. Gakusho Ki powers differ from Bushi Ki:

| Gakusho Ki Power | Cost | Effect |
|---|---|---|
| Devotion | 1 PP | +2 to the next Butsudo or Shinten roll this scene. Free action. |
| Lore Master | 2 PP | Once per session, automatically succeed with a raise on any Knowledge (Buddhism) or Knowledge (Shinto) roll. |

### Buddhist Gakusho Advancement Chain

**Novice**
- **Sacred Study** — +2 to Knowledge (Buddhism) rolls. May read and copy sutras without a Kanji roll if the text is Buddhist.
- **Compassionate Healer** — When using the *healing* power or the Healing skill, a raise removes two Wounds instead of one.

**Seasoned**
- **Gakusho Ki** — Gain 5-point Ki pool; access to Devotion and Lore Master Ki powers.
- **Temple Authority** — Group Rank within Buddhist institutions counts as 1 higher for social purposes. May perform purification rituals that reduce Pollution by 2 (standard rituals reduce by 1).
- **Dharma Master** — May teach Buddhist doctrine. Characters who study under this Gakusho for one month gain +1 to their next Knowledge (Buddhism) roll permanently.

**Veteran**
- **Yoga Scholar (Buddhist)** — Requires Dharma Master and Knowledge (Buddhism) d8+. Choose one Shugendo School. May cast that School's Novice and Seasoned powers using Butsudo. See Chapter Five: Yoga Scholar Edges for full details.
- **Sacred Texts (Advanced)** — May activate three Sacred Texts per scene (standard is one). The additional activations cost 2 PP each.

**Heroic**
- **Living Bodhisattva** — Once per session, may reduce another character's Pollution to 0 through a one-hour ceremony. May be performed at any location, not just a temple.
- **Enlightened Mind** — Immune to Fear effects. +4 to Spirit rolls against supernatural compulsion.

**Legendary**
- **Voice of the Buddha** — Once per session, speak a truth that all who hear it must acknowledge. All targets within hearing make a Spirit roll (TN 8) or are Distracted and cannot attack the Gakusho for the remainder of the scene.

## The Gakusho (Shinto) — Shinto Priest or Shrine Keeper

The Shinto Gakusho serves the Kami — the spirits of the natural world, the ancestors, and the sacred places of Nippon. They maintain ritual purity (avoiding Kegare), perform ceremonies that keep the human and spirit worlds in balance, and channel divine power through relationship with the Kami rather than through Buddhist compassion.

**Starting Skills (free):** Shinten d6, Knowledge (Shinto) d6, Notice d4, Socialize d4

**Starting Bugei (free):** None from Profession. Birth Bugei applies.

**Arcane Background (Miracles: Shinto):** Shinten d4 free, 3 starting powers from Shinto list, 10 PP.

**On Modifier:** +2

### Shinto Gakusho Advancement Chain

**Novice**
- **Kami Sense** — Passively aware of supernatural presences within Smarts" at all times. No roll required.
- **Ritual Purity** — Kegare accumulates at half the normal rate from mundane sources (contact with death, blood, etc.). Still accumulates normally from intentional transgressions.

**Seasoned**
- **Gakusho Ki** — As Buddhist version.
- **Shrine Authority** — As Temple Authority but for Shinto institutions. Purification rituals (Misogi) reduce Kegare by 2 instead of 1.
- **Purification Rite** — May perform a full Misogi purification in 30 minutes rather than the standard one hour. May purify others as well as themselves.

**Veteran**
- **Yoga Scholar (Shinto)** — Requires Purification Rite and Knowledge (Shinto) d8+. As Yoga Scholar (Buddhist) but uses Shinten as the casting skill. See Chapter Five.
- **Kami Compact** — Has made a formal agreement with a specific minor Kami. That Kami provides advance warning of supernatural threats within its domain and will not accumulate Kegare from contact with that Kami's sacred space.

**Heroic**
- **Divine Vessel** — Once per session, a Kami may speak through this Gakusho. The GM uses this to deliver information, warnings, or commands. The character has no control over when this occurs.
- **Sacred Presence** — Oni, Yurei, and other spiritually impure beings must make a Spirit roll (TN 6) to approach within 3" of this Gakusho. Failure means they cannot approach.

**Legendary**
- **Consecrate Ground** — Spend one hour in ceremony. The affected area (up to Smarts × 10 yards) becomes sacred ground. Pollution and Kegare do not accumulate there. Oni cannot enter. Yurei are automatically pushed to the edge of the area. The consecration lasts one month.

## The Sohei — Warrior Monk

The Sohei combines the martial training of the Bushi with the spiritual discipline of the Buddhist Gakusho. Temple warrior-monks who defend their religious institutions with organized force, the Sohei occupy a unique social position — they are clergy who fight, and fighters who pray, and society is never quite sure how to categorize them.

**Starting Skills (free):** Butsudo d4, Fighting d6, Intimidation d4, Knowledge (Buddhism) d4

**Starting Bugei (free):** Pole Weapons (Omote) from Profession, plus Naginatajutsu or Bojutsu (Specialist, choose one) from Profession

**Arcane Background (Miracles: Buddhist, limited):** Butsudo d4 free, 2 starting powers from Buddhist list, 10 PP.

**On Modifier:** +1

### Sohei Advancement Chain

**Novice**
- **Mushin** — As Bushi.
- **Temple Guard** — Parry +1 when stationary and defending a fixed position. Toughness +2 when defending a fixed position (does not stack with standard Cover bonus — use whichever is higher).
- **Righteous Strike** — +2 to Fighting and damage rolls against Oni, Yurei, and supernaturally evil creatures.

**Seasoned**
- **Zanshin** — As Bushi. Requires teacher.
- **Warrior's Devotion** — Pollution accumulates at half rate from combat actions taken in defense of the temple, its members, or innocent bystanders under direct threat.
- **Sacred Warrior** — May take Bushi Profession Edges from Seasoned rank onward, in addition to Sohei Edges. Does not gain Ki Focus automatically — must take it as a separate Edge.

**Veteran**
- **Fudoshin** — As Bushi. Requires teacher.
- **Exorcist's Blow** — Unarmed and staff attacks count as spiritually sanctified. May affect ethereal beings (Yurei in manifested form) without a blessed weapon.

**Heroic**
- **Shoshin** — As Bushi. Requires teacher.
- **Living Martyr** — Once per combat, when reduced to 0 Wounds (Incapacitated), make a Spirit roll (TN 4). With success, remain conscious and acting at full capability for one more round before collapsing. The Sohei fights on faith when the body fails.

**Legendary**
- **Zanshin Kanzen** — As Bushi. Requires quest.
- **Unbroken Temple** — All allied characters within Command Range gain the Righteous Strike bonus against supernatural enemies. The Sohei's presence sanctifies the battlefield.

## The Shugenja — Elemental Sorcerer

The Shugenja walks between the human world and the elemental forces that underlie it, channeling raw elemental power through the Schools of Shugendo. They are outsiders by nature — their power makes ordinary people uneasy, and they often spend extended periods in the wilderness developing their craft. A Shugenja of power is valuable and feared in equal measure.

**Starting Skills (free):** Shugendo d6, Knowledge (Supernatural Lore) d4, Notice d4, Survival d4

**Starting Bugei (free):** Pole Weapons (Omote) from Birth (Shugenja are often Buke-born). Others by Birth.

**Arcane Background (Shugendo):** Shugendo d4 free, 3 starting powers from chosen School, 10 PP.

**On Modifier:** −2

**School:** Choose one at character creation. This choice is permanent.
- **Hi-do** — School of Fire
- **Mizu-do** — School of Water
- **Hayashi-do** — School of Wood
- **Kane-do** — School of Metal
- **Tsuchi-do** — School of Soil

### Shugenja Advancement Chain

**Novice**
- **School Initiate** — Access to all Novice-rank powers from chosen School. Shugendo d4 free (from AB).
- **Elemental Sense** — Passive awareness of elemental disturbances within Smarts" related to chosen School (fire for Hi-do, water for Mizu-do, etc.). No roll required.
- **Wilderness Path** — +2 to Survival rolls. No penalty for Shugendo rolls in natural outdoor environments.

**Seasoned**
- **School Practitioner** — Access to all Seasoned-rank powers from chosen School.
- **Overreach** — May attempt to enhance a power beyond its normal parameters. Make a Spirit roll (TN 4) after casting. With success, apply one free Power Modifier at no PP cost. With failure, take 1 Fatigue. With a critical failure, the power fails and take 2 Fatigue.
- **Elemental Attunement** — +2 to Shugendo rolls when in terrain strongly associated with chosen School (near a river for Mizu-do, in a forest for Hayashi-do, on a mountain for Hi-do and Kane-do, in cultivated farmland for Tsuchi-do).

**Veteran**
- **School Mastery** — Access to all Veteran-rank powers from chosen School.
- **Yamabushi Path** — Toughness +1. Survival d8. +2 to Shugendo rolls in wilderness environments (replaces Elemental Attunement — does not stack).

**Heroic**
- **School Adept** — Access to all Heroic-rank powers from chosen School.
- **Elemental Body** — Once per scene, take on a partial elemental form for one round. During this round, gain resistance to damage of your element's type (half damage), and your melee attacks deal +1d6 elemental damage.

**Legendary**
- **School Grandmaster** — Access to all Legendary-rank powers from chosen School. May teach the School to others.
- **Elemental Avatar** — Once per session, fully manifest as an elemental being for Spirit rounds. During this time, fly (if Hi-do), breathe water (if Mizu-do), merge with vegetation (if Hayashi-do), become metallic and armored (if Kane-do), or burrow at full Pace (if Tsuchi-do). Toughness +4 during manifestation.

## The Yakuza — Criminal

The Yakuza operates outside the formal social hierarchy — a member of the criminal organizations that run gambling, protection, and vice throughout Nippon's cities. Yakuza come in many forms: the enforcer, the gambler, the go-between, the information broker. What they share is loyalty to their gang, comfort with violence, and a parallel social world with its own hierarchy and honor codes.

**Starting Skills (free):** Fighting d4, Gambling d6, Intimidation d4, Streetwise d6

**Starting Bugei (free):** Unarmed Combat (Omote) from Birth or Profession, plus Sumaijutsu (Specialist) from Profession

**On Modifier:** −3

### Yakuza Advancement Chain

**Novice**
- **Criminal Network** — May locate criminal goods, safe houses, and underworld contacts in any city with a Streetwise roll (TN 4 in familiar cities, TN 6 in new ones).
- **Pack Tactics** — +1 to Fighting rolls when two or more Yakuza gang up on the same target (in addition to the standard Gang Up bonus).
- **Street Read** — +2 to Notice rolls in urban environments. May detect surveillance (being followed or watched) with a Notice roll (TN set by the quality of the surveillance).

**Seasoned**
- **Underworld Contacts** — May call on underworld contacts twice per session for information, safe passage, or minor assistance (a place to hide, a weapon, a name).
- **Kusari-jutsu** — Requires Peasant Weapons (Omote). Chain and weighted weapons (kusari-gama, weighted chain) gain: on a raise, the target is Entangled in addition to taking damage.
- **Face** — In Yakuza social contexts, On functions normally despite the −3 Profession modifier. The Yakuza's criminal On is as real as samurai On within the underworld.

**Veteran**
- **Oyabun's Trust** — Group Rank within Yakuza organizations advances one step faster than standard. Additionally, may call on organization resources (muscle, money, safe passage) once per session without a roll.
- **Dirty Business** — Once per combat, when attacking a target who is Distracted or Vulnerable, the attack is a free action (does not count toward multi-action limit).

**Heroic**
- **Underworld Legend** — Known throughout the criminal world. All Yakuza NPCs of lower Group Rank automatically begin interactions as Friendly unless they have specific reason for hostility.
- **Shadow Operation** — May organize and execute a criminal operation (robbery, extortion, intelligence gathering) during downtime with a Streetwise roll. Success generates income or intelligence proportional to the roll result.

**Legendary**
- **Oyabun** — Has achieved the rank of gang leader. Commands a criminal organization as a living resource. The organization generates income, gathers information, and provides muscle. The exact capabilities depend on the size and scope of the gang, determined with the GM.

## The Zanshin Chain — Universal Progression

The Zanshin chain is available to all Professions, not only martial ones. It represents the mastery of focused, flowing action — reducing the cost of doing multiple things at once. A courtier who has mastered the art of simultaneous social actions is as capable of Zanshin as a swordsman.

| Edge | Rank | Requirement | Multi-Action Penalty |
|---|---|---|---|
| Mushin | Novice | — | −1 / −2 (two / three actions) |
| Zanshin | Seasoned | Mushin + teacher | −1 / −2 (same, but free counter unlocked) |
| Fudoshin | Veteran | Zanshin + teacher | −0 / −1 |
| Shoshin | Heroic | Fudoshin + teacher | −0 / −1 (free counter per round) |
| Zanshin Kanzen | Legendary | Shoshin + quest | −0 / −0 |

**Standard SWADE multi-action penalty:** −2 for two actions, −4 for three. Mushin halves this. Fudoshin eliminates it for two actions. Zanshin Kanzen eliminates all penalties.

**Teacher requirement:** Zanshin through Shoshin require a qualified teacher — a character who already possesses the Edge being learned. Finding such a teacher is itself an adventure. See Chapter Nine for teacher-finding rules.

**Zanshin Kanzen:** Cannot be taught — it must be discovered. The Game Master designs a campaign-level quest or arc appropriate to the character's path. No teacher can give you Zanshin Kanzen; they can only point toward it.
