Shugendo powers are organized by School. A Shugenja may only access powers from their chosen School — the elemental tradition they committed to at character creation. The Yoga Scholar Edge (Chapter Five) allows Gakusho to access one School's Novice and Seasoned powers through religious practice.

All Shugendo powers use the Shugendo skill (Smarts). Recovery is standard SWADE — 1 PP per hour, full on 8 hours sleep. No Pollution or Kegare accumulates from Shugendo use.

## How to Read These Tables

**SWADE Power:** Standard Savage Worlds power with Nippon trapping. Use core rules unless this appendix specifies otherwise.

**Modified:** SWADE base with Savage Bushido-specific changes. Full description appears below the table.

**New:** A power unique to Savage Bushido. Full mechanics in the description section.

**†:** Augmentation — self-targeting, long-duration buff. Standard SWADE maintenance rules apply.

## Hi-do — The School of Fire

Fire is transformation. Hi-do practitioners command purification, destruction, and the raw force of flame. They are the most overtly dangerous of the five Schools and the most socially alarming. A Shugenja who calls fire is impressive and terrifying in equal measure.

### Hi-do: Power Descriptions

**Arrows of Fire (Seasoned, 2 PP)**
SWADE Bolt with Fire trapping at extended range — Smarts × 2" rather than standard Smarts". On a raise, the struck target or a flammable object within 1" catches fire. Standard Savage Worlds fire rules apply. AP 1 from concentrated heat.

**Cloak of Smoke (Seasoned, 5 PP)**
SWADE Invisibility with Smoke trapping. The smoke column itself is visible — observers cannot track the caster within it but can see the column moving. Notice to detect the caster is at −4 rather than standard Invisibility penalty. Cannot be maintained in still enclosed spaces beyond 3 rounds without filling the area.

**Castle of Fire (Veteran, 4 PP)**
SWADE Barrier with Fire trapping. Standard Barrier mechanics. Castle Form (+2 PP): creates an enclosing ring of fire rather than a line. The ring is Smarts" in diameter. Requires Veteran rank to access Castle Form.

**Dissolving Defense (Veteran, 3 PP)**
New Power. Target one active maintained power on the opponent. Opposed Shugendo vs. the original casting roll. With success, the targeted power is suppressed for 5 rounds. With a raise, it is dispelled entirely and must be recast. Cannot target powers that have already expired or been dispelled.

**Fire Curse (Heroic, 4 PP)**
New Power. Opposed Shugendo vs. Spirit. With success, the target is Cursed — they suffer 1d4 fire damage at the start of each of their turns until the curse is removed. Removing the curse requires a successful *exorcism* or *dispel* roll against the original casting result. The curse persists indefinitely — it does not expire on its own.

## Mizu-do — The School of Water

Water flows around obstacles, finds every crack, and dissolves what resists it over time. Mizu-do practitioners excel at deception, binding, and the wearing-down of opposition. They are rarely the most dramatic fighters in a confrontation — they are the ones who determine its outcome.

### Mizu-do: Power Descriptions

**Shadow Eyes (Novice, 1 PP)**
Modified Darksight with Water trapping. As standard Darksight — no penalties in Dim or Dark conditions. Additionally, while active, the caster gains +1 to all Notice rolls involving sight. The bonus applies in normal light as well as darkness — it represents heightened visual attention, not just light amplification.

**Arrows of Water (Seasoned, 2 PP)**
SWADE Bolt with Water trapping at extended range — Smarts × 2". Force damage (no fire, no ignition). On a raise, the target is pushed back 1" and must make an Agility roll or fall Prone. AP 0 — water force is non-penetrating against armor.

**Road of Water (Seasoned, 2 PP)**
SWADE Environmental Protection with Water trapping. Duration 1 hour. The caster breathes underwater freely, is immune to pressure damage from depth, and suffers no penalties from cold water. Pace in water equals normal land Pace while active. The caster cannot breathe air while this power is active — it replaces air-breathing with water-breathing.

**Binding of Limbs (Seasoned, 2 PP)**
Modified Entangle with Water/Ice trapping. Standard Entangle on a success. On a raise the target is Bound rather than merely Entangled — they cannot move or take physical actions, and must achieve a raise on a Strength roll to break free. Fire damage of 5+ to the restraints also frees the target (the ice melts).

**Concealing Cloak (Seasoned, 5 PP)**
Modified Invisibility with Water Mist trapping. A column of water mist rises around the caster. Notice to detect is at −4 — observers see a moving mist but cannot resolve the figure inside. Unlike Cloak of Smoke, the mist does not disperse in enclosed spaces. In rain or heavy ambient mist, the trapping becomes invisible and the caster gains full standard Invisibility penalties.

**Quagmire (Veteran, 4 PP)**
Modified Entangle with area effect. Affects all targets within a Medium Blast Template centered on a target point. Each target makes a Strength roll (TN 4) or is Entangled. On a raise on the casting roll, the TN becomes 6. The affected area becomes Difficult Ground for the duration — all movement costs double. The caster may move the affected area 1" per round as a free action.

**Many Waves (Veteran, 5 PP)**
New Power. The caster creates three simultaneous water strikes. Make three separate Shugendo rolls, each against a different target (or the same target — any combination). Each successful strike deals 2d6 damage. Treat each roll independently for raises. The three strikes happen simultaneously — they do not count as multi-actions.

**Dissolving Armor (Veteran, 3 PP)**
New Power. Opposed Shugendo vs. Vigor. With success, the target's armor bonus is reduced by 1 for the duration (5 rounds). With a raise, reduced by 2. Multiple applications stack to a maximum reduction of −4. Natural armor (creature hide, supernatural toughness) is immune. The effect is purely mechanical — the armor is weakened, not visibly destroyed.

**Taming the Storm (Legendary, 8 PP)**
New Power. The caster takes full control of weather in a 1-mile radius for 1 hour. May create or dissipate rain, fog, wind, and lightning. A lightning strike deals 3d6 damage to a designated target (Shugendo roll vs. TN 6). Fog reduces visibility to 2" and all ranged attacks are at −4. Wind at gale force makes ranged attacks impossible and all movement costs double. This power cannot be rushed — requires 3 full rounds of uninterrupted casting to activate.

## Hayashi-do — The School of Wood

Wood represents growth, truth, and the patient observation of natural systems. Hayashi-do practitioners tend to be perceivers and supporters — those who reveal what is hidden, guide what grows, and sustain allies through the long campaign. In battle they are indispensable; in confrontation they prefer indirect methods.

### Hayashi-do: Power Descriptions

**Road of Wood (Seasoned, 2 PP)**
SWADE Wall Walker limited to living wood or recently cut timber. Cannot be used on old processed wood (floors, furniture older than a year). Pace on wooden surfaces equals normal Pace. The caster leaves no marks and makes no sound while Wall Walking. May hang stationary on a vertical surface or ceiling without a roll.

**Eyes of True Sight (Seasoned, 2 PP)**
Enhanced Detect/Conceal Arcana. While active, the caster automatically detects any active *illusion*, *invisibility*, or *disguise* power within Smarts" without a separate roll — the concealed figure appears as a faint luminous outline. Additionally, the caster can identify the School of any Shugendo power detected — Hi-do appears red, Mizu-do blue, Hayashi-do green, Kane-do silver, Tsuchi-do brown.

**Tongue of Beasts (Seasoned, 2 PP)**
Modified Speak Language limited to natural animals. The caster communicates verbally with any non-supernatural animal. Animals answer questions about what they have seen, heard, or smelled within their territory, within the limits of their intelligence and memory. An animal will not perform actions contrary to its instincts — a rabbit will not attack a wolf — but may share information and indicate willingness to cooperate with Beast Friend if that power is also active.

**Growth of the Lost (Seasoned, 3 PP)**
New Power. Within a Medium Blast Template, causes rapid plant growth. Choose one effect per casting:
- *Entangle:* As standard *entangle* power using Shugendo vs. Athletics.
- *Cover:* Creates Heavy Cover (+6 Armor) for all allies within the template area for the duration.
- *Barrier:* A wall of grown wood (Toughness 8) up to Smarts" long and 2" high.

**Master of the Green (Veteran, 5 PP)**
New Power. Full control of all plant life within Smarts × 2". Choose any combination of effects each round as a free action:
- Create an *entangle* effect anywhere within range.
- Create a *barrier* of rapidly grown wood (Toughness 8).
- Accelerate growth to create Difficult Ground in any zone within range.
- On a raise: cause thorny growth that deals 1d4 damage per round to any creature moving through it.

**Concealing the Trail (Veteran, 3 PP)**
New Power. Completely erases all physical evidence of passage — footprints, broken branches, scent. Covers the caster and up to five others. Duration 1 hour (retroactive — covers the last hour of travel as well as the next hour). A tracker attempting to follow the party must succeed at a Notice roll (TN 10) to find any trace at all. With a raise on the casting roll, TN becomes 12.

**Speak True (Heroic, 4 PP)**
New Power. Opposed Shugendo vs. Spirit. With success, the target must answer the next question asked of them truthfully. They know they must answer truthfully — they may refuse to answer at all (going silent or unconscious from the effort), but they cannot lie. With a raise, they must answer the next three questions truthfully. Supernatural beings with Spirit d12+ are immune.

**World Tree (Legendary, 10 PP)**
New Power. Requires 5 full rounds of uninterrupted casting. A manifestation of the World Tree's root system rises in a 3" radius around the caster. For Spirit × 2 rounds: all allies within the area heal 1 Wound at the start of each round; all enemies within the area are Entangled automatically at the start of each round (may attempt Athletics to escape each round); the caster is immune to all movement-based powers and cannot be forced to move. When the power ends, the manifestation recedes leaving no permanent change to the environment.

## Kane-do — The School of Metal

Metal is precision and control — the hardness that does not bend, the edge that divides, the lock that holds. Kane-do practitioners are masters of binding, domination, and the imposition of will on both matter and mind. In the hands of a skilled practitioner, Kane-do is less about destroying opponents than making them irrelevant.

### Kane-do: Power Descriptions

**Bar Beasts (Novice, 2 PP)**
SWADE Barrier with creature type restriction — only prevents passage by natural animals. Humans, supernatural beings, and animated objects pass freely. Animals sense the barrier and will not cross it voluntarily. Forced animals (by other characters or magic) must make a Spirit roll (TN 4) to cross, taking 1d4 damage on success (the barrier resists). Failure means they are turned back.

**Bar Men (Seasoned, 3 PP)**
SWADE Barrier with creature type restriction — only prevents passage by humans and humanoid beings. Animals, supernatural entities, and non-human creatures pass freely. A human forcing through makes a Strength roll (TN 4) and takes Str+d4 damage on a failed attempt. A raise on the casting roll raises the Strength TN to 6.

**Bar Missiles (Heroic, 3 PP)**
SWADE Deflection enhanced for ranged attacks specifically. Provides −4 to all ranged attacks against the target (rather than the standard −2). Additionally, on a critical failure of the ranged attack, the deflected missile returns toward the attacker — the attacker makes an Agility roll or takes 1d4 damage from their own deflected weapon.

**Bar Spirits (Veteran, 3 PP)**
SWADE Arcane Protection specifically against supernatural beings — Kami, Oni, Yurei, Kitsune, and all beings with supernatural origin. Affected beings cannot enter the warded area without making an opposed Spirit roll against the caster's Shugendo casting result. On failure they are barred; on success they enter but take 2d4 damage from the cold iron force. Maintained.

**Mutability of Form (Heroic, 4 PP)**
Modified Shape Change with Metal trapping. The caster transforms into any metal object of roughly similar mass — a sword, a door latch, a suit of armor on a rack. While in object form: immune to physical damage; cannot be detected as alive by mundane observation; maintains full awareness of surroundings; cannot cast powers or communicate; cannot be targeted by hostile powers (appears as an ordinary object). Returning to human form is a free action. The transformation is visually perfect — detecting the disguise requires a raise on a Notice roll (TN 8) or an active *detect/conceal arcana* power.

**Iron World (Legendary, 10 PP)**
New Power. Requires 3 rounds of casting. Within a Large Blast Template centered on the caster, all surfaces (floor, walls, any structure) become metal for the duration. Effects: all movement within the area costs double (slick metal floors); creatures without metal-appropriate footwear make Agility rolls (TN 4) each round or fall Prone; the caster and any designated allies are immune to the movement penalty; any fire-based power is extinguished on contact with the metallic surfaces (fire has no fuel); Tsuchi-do and Hayashi-do powers are suppressed within the area (metal dominates both earth and wood).

## Tsuchi-do — The School of Soil

### Tsuchi-do: Power Descriptions

**Road of Soil (Seasoned, 3 PP)**
SWADE Burrow limited to natural earth, packed soil, clay, sand, and soft rock. Cannot move through worked stone (paved roads, castle walls, mortared floors). Pace underground equals normal Pace. No tunnel is left — the earth closes behind. May surface at any point within movement range as a free action. Emerging in the same space as an enemy counts as The Drop.

**Fronting the Storm (Seasoned, 3 PP)**
Modified Protection with Earth trapping. Provides +4 Armor rather than the standard +2 — the enduring nature of earth. However, the caster's Pace is reduced by 1 while maintained (the earth-force slows movement). On a raise on the casting roll, Armor is +6 with the same Pace reduction.

**The Angry Earth (Veteran, 5 PP)**
New Power. A localized earthquake affecting a Large Blast Template centered on the target point. Every creature in the affected area makes an Agility roll (TN 6) or is knocked Prone and takes 2d6 damage from the violent ground movement. Structures within the area take 3d6 damage applied to their Toughness. The ground becomes Difficult Ground for 10 minutes after the quake. The caster is immune if standing within the area.

**Armor Invincible (Heroic, 5 PP)**
Modified Armor with very strong Earth trapping. Provides +8 Armor while maintained. The caster's Pace is reduced by 2 and they cannot Run — the stone weight is significant. On a raise, Armor is +10. Against weapons with AP equal to or exceeding the Armor value, this power still grants +2 minimum — even AP weapons leave traces of stone protection. The caster's skin takes on a granite-like texture that is unmistakable.

**End of Sorcery (Veteran, 5 PP)**
New Power. The caster makes a Shugendo roll. With success, every active maintained power within a Medium Blast Template centered on the target point is dispelled — including the caster's own maintained powers. The dispelling affects powers from all traditions equally. Powers that are instantaneous (already resolved) are unaffected. This power cannot be maintained — it fires and resolves immediately. On a raise, the area extends to a Large Blast Template.

**Living Mountain (Legendary, 10 PP)**
New Power. The caster partially transforms into an earth elemental for Spirit rounds. During transformation: Size increases by 2 (Toughness +2); Strength increases by two die types; all physical attacks deal +1d8 earth damage; the caster is immune to *entangle*, *quagmire*, and all movement-restriction powers; Pace drops to 4 but the caster may Burrow at Pace 8 as a free action each round. When the power ends, the caster returns to normal size and loses all bonuses. Takes 1 Fatigue from the transformation effort regardless of outcome.

## Yoga Scholar — Cross-Tradition Access

The Yoga Scholar Edge (Chapter Five) allows Gakusho of either Buddhist or Shinto tradition to access one School's Novice and Seasoned powers, cast through Butsudo or Shinten respectively.

**Access limit:** Yoga Scholar grants access to Novice and Seasoned rank powers only. Veteran, Heroic, and Legendary powers remain exclusive to full Shugenja practitioners.

**Casting skill:** The Gakusho uses their own arcane skill (Butsudo or Shinten), not Shugendo. This means the power's effectiveness is capped by the Gakusho's arcane skill level rather than the Shugenja's.

**Spiritual cost:** Powers cast through Yoga Scholar still accumulate Pollution (Buddhist) or Kegare (Shinto) under standard rules. The elemental nature of the power does not exempt the practitioner from their tradition's spiritual accounting.

**Trapping:** All powers carry the Gakusho's religious trapping — a Buddhist Yoga Scholar casting Hi-do fire powers produces sacred golden flame rather than raw elemental fire. The mechanical effect is identical; the appearance and spiritual character differ.

| School | Buddhist Trapping | Shinto Trapping |
|---|---|---|
| Hi-do | Purifying wisdom-flame of the Buddha | Fire Kami's blessing — divine rather than elemental |
| Mizu-do | Flow of compassion — water as mercy | River Kami's gift — cool and clear |
| Hayashi-do | Growth of the Bodhi tree — sacred wood | Forest Kami's favor — living and aware |
| Kane-do | Precision of enlightened mind — clarity | Mountain Kami's hardness — ancient and unyielding |
| Tsuchi-do | Groundedness of meditation — firm foundation | Earth Kami's patience — enduring and sustaining |
