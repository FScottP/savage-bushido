This appendix provides Game Master reference stat blocks for humans and beasts a party may encounter in Savage Bushido. All stats use SWADE format. Wild Cards are marked [WC] and have a Wild Die; Extras do not.

Stat blocks represent average members of each type. Adjust freely — a veteran Ashigaru with twenty campaigns behind him differs significantly from a raw conscript. Use these as baselines.

## Reading the Stat Blocks

Attributes and Skills follow standard SWADE. All setting skills from Chapter Three apply. On Score is listed for human NPCs with meaningful social standing — Extras with no listed On have On 4 by default. Pace; Parry; Toughness follow PEG stat block convention with semicolons between derived stats. Armor is factored into Toughness and noted in parentheses. Wild Cards have Hindrances, Edges, and Gear entries even when empty (shown as —).

## Warriors

### Ashigaru — Foot Soldier
*Extra — Heimin Bushi (basic infantry)*

**Attributes:** Agility d6, Smarts d4, Spirit d6, Strength d6, Vigor d6
**Skills:** Athletics d6, Fighting d6, Intimidation d4, Notice d4, Shooting d4
**Pace** 6; **Parry** 5; **Toughness** 7 (2); **On** 4
**Edges:** Pole Weapons (Omote)
**Gear:** Yari (Str+d6, Reach 1); Tanto (Str+d4); optional Tanegashima arquebus (2d8, AP 2, Range 10/20/40, RoF 1).
**Special Abilities:**
- **Armor +2:** Light Ashigaru armor.

*The backbone of every army in Nippon. Ashigaru in organized formations use the Gang Up bonus freely and fight with disciplined spear walls. Routed Ashigaru are desperate men with spears.*

## Beasts of Nippon

The natural world of Nippon contains formidable creatures. Mountain forests hide bears and wolves; coastal waters shelter sharks; the mountains are home to giant boars and territorial deer.

---

### Wild Boar (Inoshishi)
*Extra — Beast*

**Attributes:** Agility d6, Smarts d4 (A), Spirit d8, Strength d10, Vigor d8
**Skills:** Athletics d6, Fighting d6, Notice d6
**Pace** 7; **Parry** 5; **Toughness** 8 (1)
**Special Abilities:**
- **Armor +1:** Thick hide.
- **Charge:** If the boar moves at least 3" before attacking, it adds +4 to damage.
- **Frenzy:** May make two Fighting attacks at −2.
- **Low Light Vision:** No penalty in Dim or Dark conditions.
- **Tusks:** Str+d4.

*A charging boar can overturn a horse and impale a warrior. Yamabushi pay close attention to signs of boar territory.*

---

### Black Bear (Kuma)
*Extra — Beast*

**Attributes:** Agility d6, Smarts d6 (A), Spirit d8, Strength d12, Vigor d10
**Skills:** Athletics d8, Fighting d8, Intimidation d8, Notice d8
**Pace** 8; **Parry** 6; **Toughness** 10
**Edges:** Frenzy
**Special Abilities:**
- **Bite/Claw:** Str+d6.
- **Grapple:** +2 to Grapple rolls; a grappled target takes Str+d4 crushing damage at the start of each subsequent turn until they escape.
- **Hardy:** A second Shaken result doesn't cause a Wound.
- **Low Light Vision.**
- **Size 2:** Adult bears weigh 300–500 lbs.

*Sacred in Ainu tradition and feared in Nipponese farming communities. Bears who have learned humans carry food are far more dangerous than wild ones.*
